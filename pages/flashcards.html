How to use

  • 5-min daily loop — pick a category, flip 10 cards, mark Known/Unknown honestly.
  • Pre-interview cram — filter by the topic you'll be grilled on; hide Known to focus on weak spots.
  • Spaced practice — come back weekly; cards you marked Unknown will keep showing up until they stick.
Pro tip: if you can't explain the answer in your own words to a rubber duck, mark it Unknown — recognition ≠ recall.
